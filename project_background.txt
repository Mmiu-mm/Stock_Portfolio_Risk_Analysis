## Project Overview

**The Challenge:**
China's stock market is evolving rapidly, creating complex investment environments. Traditional methods lack systematic risk quantification tools.

**Our Solution:**
A comprehensive stock portfolio risk analysis system applying modern financial theory to investment decisions.

**Dataset Scope:**
- **6 Representative A-Shares:** Sanqi Mutual Entertainment, Perfect World, Yongyou Network, Kingsoft Office, ZTE Corporation, Luxshare Precision
- **Time Coverage:** September 2016 - August 2025
- **Market Representation:** Cross-industry coverage with varied market capitalizations

**Key Objectives:**
Build complete portfolio risk analysis system  
Apply modern portfolio theory to Chinese markets  
Develop intuitive risk visualization dashboard  
Provide scientific decision support tools
